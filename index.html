THOUGHT BEHIND GRAHITA

Grahita: As within, so without

Grahita in Sanskrit means receptor, the one who recieves, accepts. People need someone who can listen. Grahita, as a psychological assistance is committed towards being an active listener and serves the society by doing the best. Our perception, thoughts and feelings create what we see and experience. Therefore, Grahita focuses on our internal world and cultivating a positive relationship with ourselves. We believe that, the way we exercise to keep us physically fit, it is essential to train our mind as well. Grahita is a place where we not only work on the distress but also pursue personal growth and development.  Grahita offers everyone a safe space where you are welcomed, listened and understood!
